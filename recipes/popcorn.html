Tasty Vanilla Muffins Description

Few things evoke the cozy feeling of a movie night quite like the aroma of freshly popped popcorn.

Whether you crave a simple buttery treat or a sweet and salty indulgence, this guide will walk you through the secrets to acheiving popcorn perfection.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Popcorn kernels
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Butter
  • Caramel

Steps

  1. Put the popcorn kernels in a bowl or paper bag.
  2. Add some oil and mix
  3. Heat it up and allow them to pop
  4. Add flavor using salt or sugar or butter or caramel
