Tasty Vanilla Muffins Description

Is there anything more comforting than the aroma of freshly baked vanilla muffins wafting through the kitchen?

This recipe delivers perfectly moist, fluffy muffins with a delightful vanilla flavor that's simple to make and irresistable to eat.These vanilla muffins come together in minutes with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingridients in one bowl
  2. Mix wet ingredients in another bowl
  3. Add the wet and dry ingredients together
  4. Put the mixture in the oven.
