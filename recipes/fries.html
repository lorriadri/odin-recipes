Fries Description

Beyond the basic potato lies a world of flavor possibilities waiting to be unlocked.

Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and seasoned to perfection - fries are the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes.
  2. Heat the oil and put the potatoes into the hot oil.
  3. Remove the potatoes when golden crispy brown.
  4. Put salt and spices to add flavor.
